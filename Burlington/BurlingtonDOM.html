Top Burlington Neighbourhoods in May 2021

 

In this post I will show you the most popular neighbourhoods in Burlington in May 2021.

 

The most popular neighbourhoods are the ones where houses don't last long on the market! For that reason, I'll be using Average Days on Market as my statistic to rank the Burlington neighbourhoods. Average Days on Market will tell you how long (on average) these homes last on the market before they are sold!

 

Since each Burlington neighbourhood is unique in what it offers, I have broken down the rankings into Detached, Semi Detached, Townhouses, Condo Apartments and Condo Townhouses. This way you can see which Burlington neighbourhood is most popular for your type of home (or the one you are looking for!). I do also provide overall rankings as well at the end this post.
